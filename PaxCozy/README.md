#  Luggage Scanner

A web-based application developed by a group of contributors, including [Cathy Wong](https://www.linkedin.com/in/cathy-wong-711731334) , [Yanny Liu](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yanny-liuyanlui/),  and [Zoe Li](https://www.linkedin.com/in/zoeliunfai/), to help travelers check the safety and compliance of luggage items with aviation regulations. The application supports real-time camera scanning and text-based item checks, providing detailed information on whether items are allowed, restricted, or prohibited based on travel routes.

## Features
- **Travel Route Selection**: Choose departure and destination to check regulations specific to your journey.
- **Live Camera Detection**: Use your device's camera to scan items in real-time (simulated AI detection).
- **Text-Based Item Check**: Enter item names (e.g., "perfume") to verify compliance.
- **Quick Check for Common Items**: Predefined buttons for items like power banks, laptops, and liquids.
- **Recent Scan History**: View and manage past scan records.
- **Responsive Design**: Built with Tailwind CSS for a mobile-friendly interface.
- **Regulation Details**: Provides conditions, restrictions, and travel tips for scanned items.
